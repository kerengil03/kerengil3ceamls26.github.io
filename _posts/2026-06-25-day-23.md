  Today, our group focused primarily on preparing for tomorrow's presentation. We also timed ourselves during the practice sessions to make sure that our presentation fit within the required time limit. Practicing as a group helped us identify areas where we needed to provide more explanation or simplify certain concepts. Overall, today's preparation helped me feel more confident in both my understanding of the research and my ability to present it effectively during tomorrow's presentation.
