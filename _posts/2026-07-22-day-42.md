  Today, our group continued preparing for the Final Symposium by reviewing the presentation one last time and officially assigning everyone's speaking roles. We walked through the presentation from beginning to end, discussing transitions between speakers and making sure the flow felt natural. We also reviewed the content on each slide to ensure everyone was comfortable explaining their assigned sections and answering potential questions. As we practiced, we gave each other feedback on pacing, clarity, and confidence to strengthen our overall presentation. We made a few final adjustments to our scripts and confirmed that all of our visuals matched what we planned to discuss. By the end of the day, everyone had a clear understanding of their role and felt more prepared for the symposium. Overall, today's work helped us become more organized and confident as we get ready to present our research.
