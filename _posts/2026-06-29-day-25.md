  Today was the day of our Mid-Symposium presentation. Before presenting, my group and I reviewed our slides one last time to make sure everyone was prepared and understood their sections. I also did a better job explaining my slides and understanding the information I was presenting instead of just reading from the screen. Presenting today helped me realize how much I have learned throughout this program. I also enjoyed listening to the other groups and learning more about their research projects. Overall, today's presentation helped strengthen both my communication and presentation skills.
