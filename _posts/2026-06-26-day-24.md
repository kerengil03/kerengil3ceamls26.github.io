what_i_learned: |
  Today, our group continued rehearsing our presentation in preparation for the upcoming Mid-Symposium. Compared to yesterday, I felt that our presentation went much smoother because we were more familiar with the material and were able to explain our sections with greater confidence. Practicing multiple times allowed us to speak more naturally about our research rather than relying entirely on our scripts. Before combining with the other research groups for a larger rehearsal, we spent time practicing within our own group and providing one another with feedback. This helped us identify areas where we needed to elaborate more on certain concepts and improve the transitions between slides. I noticed that I have become more comfortable discussing my contributions to the project and explaining the technical concepts involved in our research. Although I have improved since yesterday, I still want to continue practicing over the weekend before the Mid-Symposium. I would like to work on projecting my voice more clearly so that everyone in the audience can hear me and also practice maintaining eye contact and facing the audience while presenting. Overall, today's rehearsal helped me become more confident in my presentation skills and showed me the areas where I can continue improving before the symposium.

I also want to continue improving my ability to face the audience instead of focusing too much on my slides or notes. However, repeated practice and feedback from my group members helped me become more aware of these areas and gave me a better idea of what to focus on before the Mid-Symposium.
